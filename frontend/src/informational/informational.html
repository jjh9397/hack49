Understanding Epilepsy

What is Epilepsy?

Epilepsy is a neurological disorder characterized by recurrent seizures...

Types of Seizures

  • Focal Seizures: Seizures that occur in one part of the brain...
  • Generalized Seizures: Seizures that affect both sides of the brain...
  • Tonic-Clonic Seizures: Characterized by muscle stiffening and jerking...
  • Absence Seizures: Brief lapses in consciousness...
  • Myoclonic Seizures: Sudden jerks or twitches...
  • Atonic Seizures: Sudden loss of muscle tone...

Causes of Epilepsy

Various potential causes include...

  • Genetic Factors: Family history and hereditary conditions...
  • Structural Abnormalities: Brain injuries, tumors...
  • Infections: Meningitis, encephalitis...
  • Metabolic Disorders: Imbalances in electrolytes...
  • Developmental Disorders: Conditions like autism...
