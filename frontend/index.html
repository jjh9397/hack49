What is Epilepsy?

Epilepsy is a chronic disorder where individuals experience recurrent, unprovoked seizures. Seizures are disruptions in the brain’s electrical activity and can vary in intensity and duration.

Learn more about the causes, types, and how to manage epilepsy effectively. It can be caused by genetic factors, brain injuries, or certain illnesses.
